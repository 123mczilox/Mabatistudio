Gauge Differences

Gauge 28 vs 30

Choose the right thickness for your budget and performance needs.

  • Gauge 28: thicker, stronger, more durable, premium quality.
  • Gauge 30: lighter, more affordable, ideal for standard residential roofs.
Matte Finish

Modern premium appearance

Matte finishes offer a grounded aesthetic with subtle texture.

  • Less reflective in bright sunlight
  • Hides scratches and marks better
  • Elegant, understated architectural tone
Gloss Finish

Reflective vibrant look

Gloss finishes create a polished, classic mabati statement.

  • Shiny reflective surface
  • Classic mabati aesthetic
  • Easy to wipe clean and maintain
